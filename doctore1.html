About Dr. NP Mishra

Dr. NP Mishra is a board-certified skin specialist with over 20 years of experience in dermatology. He is dedicated to providing the best care for his patients, offering advanced treatments and personalized care for various skin conditions.

Dr. Mishra completed his medical education at Harvard Medical School and further specialized in dermatology during his residency at the Mayo Clinic. He is highly experienced in treating a range of skin conditions including acne, eczema, and psoriasis. Dr. Mishra is committed to staying current with the latest advancements in skin care and treatment, ensuring his patients receive the most effective and up-to-date care possible.
